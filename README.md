---

## x01 - The Problem

The original issue that Pinespark aims to solve is the increasing price of AI and AI infrastructure. As prices for GPUs and RAM continue to skyrocket, AI, even open-weight models, is becoming harder and harder to access. Pinespark's goal is to fix this by providing low-cost, and, in some cases, free access to AI so that AI technology is accessible to all.
